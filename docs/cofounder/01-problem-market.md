The switch from dopamine-rich social media to deep work creates an unbearable neurological crash for 15–30-year-olds.

**Existing tools** (Notion, habit trackers, generic AI) are either flat, fragmented, or lack emotional intelligence. None combine **gamification + persistent AI memory**.

---

### The Core Problem

#### The dopamine gap

TikTok, gaming, Instagram: constant feedback, reward, stimulation.

Then: deep work, alone, in silence, no signal that anything is working.

The gap between those two states is where motivation collapses.

<aside>
📉

- 1 in 7 people aged 15–30 now live with a mental health condition (OMS).
- Gen Z emotional distress is double that of millennials (McKinsey).
- Students and entrepreneurs spend most of their time alone in front of a screen.
- Life progression stalls while expectations keep rising.
</aside>

---

### The thesis

You can’t eliminate dopamine. So inject it into the work.

Make every hour of deep work generate **visible progression**:

- **XP**
- **Ranks**
- **Quests**
- **Skill trees**

The brain responds the same way it does to a game, because the mechanics are identical.

- **Pokémon GO** proved this at mass scale for movement.
- **Strava** proved it for sport.
- **Promethee** applies it to cognitive work and personal development.

### Competitive Landscape

#### Replika

- 30M users
- Emotional AI companion
- Only 10% of interactions contextualised beyond 24h
- Oriented toward the intimate, weak in work and productivity
- No gamification

#### Friend

- AI pendant with passive mic
- GDPR-incompatible in Europe
- Responses are generic
- No memory beyond 30 seconds
- No identified use case
- Perceived as a curiosity with no real utility

#### Notion / Whoop

- Fragmented
- Heavy setup
- No real gamification
- No AI mentor
- Require manual input and parameterisation
- Solve a different problem

#### Generic LLMs

- No persistent user memory
- No specialisation in psychological coaching
- Require advanced prompting
- No emotional intelligence
- Users don’t integrate them into daily rituals

---

### Where Promethee wins

No one combines:

- **OS-level overlay**
- **Persistent AI memory**
- **Behavioural gamification**
- **Community layer**
- **Zero-prompt AI interaction**

<aside>
🏰

**Moat:** centralisation + accumulated personal data + the psychological cost of leaving.

Your XP, your skill tree, your history: all gone if you switch.

</aside>

### Why Now

- LLM maturity makes a real emotionally intelligent mentor technically achievable.
- “Self-improvement” culture is at a generational peak.
- AI integration in daily non-professional life is still embryonic.
- The distribution machine (X, YouTube, Discord) is already built and generating revenue.
